Background

I’m a Computer Science freshman, a highly-motivated mobile and web developer with an acquired taste for elegant design, an open source hobbyist passionate about analyzing and matching underlying patterns within our diverse ideas so that we don’t have to keep reinventing the wheel and lastly, a community mentor keen to inspire change-makers.

Being a full-stack engineer, my goal is to always build applications that are scalable and efficient under the hood while providing engaging, adaptive, eye-candy user experiences. On the other hand, community gives me a platform to share complementary ideas with my peers and accelerate the process of crafting better solutions without being distracted by the buzz, glitz and opportunities that seem to exist.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm probably talking at a tech meetup, hanging out with my bestfriend or giving free therapy to a cool friend.
